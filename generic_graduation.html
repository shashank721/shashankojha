The Foundation: School Years

My fascination with mathematics and science began in my early school years. The thrill of solving complex problems, understanding the laws of nature, and unraveling the mysteries of science always captured my imagination. It was this childhood curiosity that set the stage for my educational journey.

During my adolescence (Teenage), I observed a significant upsurge in the demand for skilled professionals, particularly in technical domains. B.Tech. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering

In 2017, I embarked on my B.Tech. journey, specializing in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at RRSIMT. The curriculum was rigorous and demanding, but it was also incredibly rewarding. Throughout my undergraduate years, I dived deep into the principles and applications of electrical and electronics engineering, honing my skills in designing, analyzing, and troubleshooting electrical systems. My CGPA of 7.28 reflects the dedication and hard work I put into my studies.
